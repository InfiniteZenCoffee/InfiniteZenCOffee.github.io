Infinte Zen Coffee Co. & Noshery

In 2015, we launched Infinite Zen Coffee Co. as the first mobile coffee shop in Northwest Ohio. In a short time, we realized that we needed to expand to better serve our customers.
We are now a full service coffee shop on wheels, complete with a kitchen to satisfy hunger while we continue to caffeinate the tired and thirsty masses. Our fare aims to be fresh and light and locally sourced when possible. Our baked goods are still made from scratch with love.
The happy coffee camper has grown into a coffee bus allowing us to expand our offerings to better serve you!
